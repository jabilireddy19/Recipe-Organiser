Chicken Parmesan Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  2. 1 cup breadcrumbs
  3. 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  4. 1 egg
  5. 1 cup marinara sauce
  6. 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper
  3. Dip each breast in beaten egg, then coat in breadcrumb mixture
  4. Place coated chicken breasts on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through
  6. Top each breast with marinara sauce and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese
  7. Return to oven and bake for an additional 5-10 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly

Vegetable Stir Fry Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  2. 1 small onion, diced
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 cup broccoli florets
  5. 1 cup carrots, peeled and sliced
  6. 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  7. 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  8. 1 teaspoon honey
  9. 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat
  2. Add diced onion and minced garlic and sauté until softened
  3. Add broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers and stir fry for 3-4 minutes
  4. Add soy sauce, honey, and red pepper flakes and stir fry for an additional 2-3 minutes
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  1. 2 large eggs
  2. 1 cup (200g) all-purpose flour
  3. 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  4. 6 oz (170g) best-quality bittersweet or semisweet chocolate, chopped
  5. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. 1/2 cup (120ml) unsalted butter, at room temperature

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Butter six 6-ounce ramekins or small cups and dust them with sugar.
  3. Melt chocolate in a double boiler or in the microwave in 30-second increments, stirring between each heating.
  4. Whisk together flour and 1/2 cup of sugar. Add eggs one at a time, whisking well after each addition.
  5. Add chocolate and whisk until smooth.
  6. Pour batter into prepared ramekins, filling them about 3/4 of the way full.
  7. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, or until edges are set and centers are still slightly jiggly.

Summer Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups mixed summer berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, blackberries)
  2. 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  3. 1 cup sliced red onion
  4. 1 avocado, diced
  5. 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint
  6. Juice of 1 lemon
  7. 1 tablespoon honey

Steps:

  1. Combine berries, cherry tomatoes, sliced red onion, and avocado in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk together lemon juice and honey in a small bowl.
  3. Pour dressing over the fruit and toss gently to combine.
  4. Sprinkle with mint and serve immediately.
